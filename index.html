Cloud City

Manage a floating colony in the clouds of Venus. Survive 12 orbital cycles.

Cloud City is a real-time resource management strategy game set high in the clouds of Venus.

You command a floating platform colony suspended above the scorching Venusian surface. Your mission: keep the colony alive through 12 full orbital cycles — 60 minutes of real time.

Features

  • Manage critical life-support systems — balance Air, Power, and Food. Let any one reach zero and the colony falls.
  • Build and expand your colony — construct Air Harvesters, Solar Factories, Farms, Balloon Factories, and more.
  • Research advanced technologies — unlock Hydroponic Farms, Bigger Blimps, and Solid-State Batteries.
  • Explore the solar system — 3D visualisation of Venus and the solar system rendered in real time.
