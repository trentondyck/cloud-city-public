# Cloud City

Manage a floating colony in the clouds of Venus. Survive 12 orbital cycles.

Cloud City is a real-time resource management strategy game set high in the clouds of Venus.

You command a floating platform colony suspended above the scorching Venusian surface. Your mission: keep the colony alive through 12 full orbital cycles — 60 minutes of real time.

**Manage critical life-support systems**
Balance three vital resources: Air (breathable atmosphere), Power (solar energy), and Food (hydroponics). Let any one reach zero and the colony falls. Lose enough lift and your platforms sink into the inferno below.

**Build and expand your colony**
Construct factories to harvest what you need:
- Air Harvesters — extract breathable gases from the Venusian atmosphere
- Solar Factories & Batteries — generate and store electrical power
- Platform Factories — expand your colony's footprint in the clouds
- Balloon Factories — maintain the lift keeping everything airborne
- Carbon & Sulfur Harvesters — gather raw materials for research
- Farms — grow food to feed your growing settlement

**Research advanced technologies**
Unlock three game-changing upgrades using harvested materials:
- Hydroponic Farms — reduce food consumption by 50%
- Bigger Blimps — cut balloon requirements in half
- Solid-State Batteries — dramatically boost power storage capacity

**Explore the solar system**
Switch to Colony View for a 3D visualisation of Venus and the full solar system rendered in real time. Watch planets orbit the sun as your colony fights for survival in the clouds above.

**Four ways to lose**
Air depleted, food exhausted, power grid collapsed, or colony loses lift and falls. Keep all systems green, manage your factories wisely, and survive long enough to claim VICTORY.

**Remove Ads**
A one-time in-app purchase removes banner ads and supports development.

**No permissions required**
Cloud City requires no device permissions. No camera, no microphone, no location, no internet connection needed to play. Fully offline.
